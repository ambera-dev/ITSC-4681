About

This tool was created by Amber Atkins and Max Carey for their Capstone Project at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte.
The application displays the output of tools such as nmap (a network scanner), TShark (a terminal oriented version of Wireshark, a network protocol analyzer), and tcpdump (a packet analyzer).
Assistance and oversight by Nadia Najjar.
